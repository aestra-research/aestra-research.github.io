WCET4 · Denver 2026

Two contributions to the 4th World Congress for Existential Therapy

Graham Nelson-Zutter, MSc, is presenting two pieces of work at the 4th World Congress for Existential Therapy (WCET4) in Denver, June 2026 - one experiential, one methodological. Open materials for both are gathered here.

Sessions

Guided Meditation Workshop · Längle's Four Fundamental Motivations

An experiential workshop translating Längle's Four Fundamental Motivations into four guided meditations, each anchored in concrete imagery. Participants move through the meditation and then name what they noticed before each Fundamental Existential Prerequisite is revealed.

Thursday, June 4, 2026 · 11:00 am · Workshop materials & take-home recordings →

"Do You Understand?!" Best Practices using AI in Research (and Life)

A methodology paper on multi-LLM cross-validation, quote-anchored source verification, and iterative re-anchoring - illustrated by two open-source Existential Analysis codebooks built with those techniques.

Saturday, June 6, 2026 · 3:00 pm · Companion codebooks (CC BY 4.0) →

By Graham Nelson-Zutter, MSc student, Existential Analysis & Logotherapy, University of Salzburg.
