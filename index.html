EA Codebooks for LLM-Assisted Qualitative Analysis

Two open-source codebooks for scoring participant testimony against Längle's frameworks (Four Fundamental Motivations and Twelve Fundamental Existential Prerequisites), built by multi-LLM cross-validation against Längle source material. Designed for use as LLM constraints in qualitative research.

Companion to the WCET4 (2026) paper "Do You Understand?!" Best Practices using Artificial Intelligence in Research (and Life). CC BY 4.0.

By Graham Nelson-Zutter, MSc student, Existential Analysis & Logotherapy, University of Salzburg.
